How we engage

Three ways to work together.

Most clients start with the Assessment, then move into the Fractional AI Strategy Officer engagement. AI Advisory is reserved for graduates.

Standalone Diagnostic

AI Operations Assessment

3 weeks · Fixed scope

A focused diagnostic to identify where AI creates the most value in your business — and exactly how to capture it.

  • Assessment of current tools, AI readiness, and team skill across identified GTM and operations leads
  • Prioritized roadmap of the top AI opportunities — what to build, in what order, and why
  • Recommended tools, implementation approach, and cost estimates for each priority workstream
  • Ownership map — leads, support, and internal capacity required per workstream
The Main Work
Embedded Engagement

Fractional AI Strategy Officer

3 - 6 months

We embed as your Fractional AI Strategy Officer, paired with named functional leads across your business. Together we architect the highest-leverage AI workflows — and your team builds them with us, hands-on.

  • Tool selection and vendor evaluation
  • AI roadmap ownership and prioritization
  • Hands-on architecture across AI workflows
  • Skill and connector configuration
  • Weekly working sessions with named lead(s)
  • Biweekly leadership check-ins
  • Internal training and capability transfer
  • Async support throughout
Post Engagement

AI Advisory

Continuing

Once your team is running the AI playbook, we stay on as a sounding board without the embedded cadence. Keep your AI work aligned to business value as you scale.

  • Biweekly strategy calls
  • Priority async support
  • Quarterly roadmap reviews
